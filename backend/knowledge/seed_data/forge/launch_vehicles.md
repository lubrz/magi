# Launch Vehicle Comparison

Current and near-future launch vehicles span a wide range of capability and cost. Falcon 9: 22.8t to LEO, $67M, partially reusable (booster). Falcon Heavy: 63.8t to LEO, $150M, partially reusable. SLS Block 1: 95t to LEO, $2.2B, fully expendable. Starship: 150+t to LEO, projected $10-50M, fully reusable (unproven). Vulcan Centaur: 27.2t to LEO, ~$120M, expendable. New Glenn: 45t to LEO, pricing TBD, partially reusable. Ariane 6: 21.6t to LEO, ~$80M, expendable. The trend is clear: reusability dominates the cost equation. Any major space programme architecture should assume reusable launch in the 2030s baseline, not expendable.
